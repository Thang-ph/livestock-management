# Livestock Management System

A web-based livestock management system designed to help farming teams monitor animal health, vaccination schedules, and operational activities across farms.  
The system improves coordination between farmers, technicians, and managers by providing centralized data tracking and operational dashboards.

---

## Overview

Livestock farming operations often require accurate tracking of animal health, vaccination schedules, and field activities.  
This system provides a centralized platform to manage these processes efficiently, enabling real-time monitoring and better decision-making.

Key objectives of the system:

- Track livestock health records
- Manage vaccination schedules
- Monitor farm operations
- Provide statistical reports and operational dashboards
- Improve collaboration between farm staff and management
